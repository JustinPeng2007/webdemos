Who We Are

Spark is a non-profit organization based in Canada that is dedicated to empowering high school students to pursue their passions and make a positive impact in their communities. Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are committed to providing young people with the tools, resources, and opportunities they need to succeed.

Our Mission

At Spark, we are dedicated to empowering Canadian high school students to pursue their passions and unlock their potential. As part of this commitment, we are proud to announce that we have established the first-ever platform for Canadian High school students to connect with local companies or other Not-for-profit organizations for internships in the fields of STEM and innovation.
